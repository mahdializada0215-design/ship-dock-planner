📦 PACK — FLEX TRIGGERS

Move HC to Multi

  • Multi WIP > 30 min of work
  • Singles WIP < 15 min (starving)
  • Multi CPTs approaching
  • Multi backlog from upstream

Move HC to Singles

  • Singles WIP > 30 min of work
  • Multi WIP < 15 min (drying up)
  • End of shift — flush singles
  • Singles CPTs at risk
