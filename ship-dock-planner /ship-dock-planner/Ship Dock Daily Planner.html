HOW IT WORKS

1. Multis reduce effective volume: 500 multis @ 2.5 items/box = 1250 items packed in 500 boxes, saving 750 from target.
2. Fluid units handled by FL team (100 UPH, 9 productive hrs).
3. Pallet team handles remaining volume at 72 UPH with 0.5hr for start-ups & tidiness (9 productive hrs).
4. Pallet capacity per HC = 72 UPH x 9 hrs = 648 pkgs/shift.
5. FL capacity per HC = 100 UPH x 9 hrs = 900 units/shift.
6. Loaders have 30% capacity to help palletize = 21.6 UPH x 9 hrs = 194 units/shift per loader (only when target < 5,000 AND PROD HRS > 2).
7. If pallet team has surplus, those spare hours flex to fluid at FL UPH.
8. If Fluid HC has spare capacity (fluid units < FL capacity), free hours go to lines at Palletizing UPH. E.g. 1 FL HC with 400 units (cap 900) = 500 surplus ÷ 100 = 5 hrs free x 72 = 360 extra line units.
